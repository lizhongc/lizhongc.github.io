Short Bio

I am a research officer in the Prof. Gordon K Smyth's lab team at the Bioinformatics Division, Walter and Eliza Hall Institute of Medical Research (WEHI). I have submitted the Ph.D. thesis on Feb 2022 and will receive the Ph.D. degree in Statistics from the University of Melbourne around May 2022. I received my M.S. and B.S. in Mathematics from Peking University on Jun. 2016 and Jul. 2013, respectively.
