About me

I am a research officer in Prof. Gordon K Smyth lab team at Bioinformatics Division, Walter and Eliza Hall Institute of Medical Research (WEHI). I completed my PhD degree in Statistics from the University of Melbourne under the supervision of A/Prof. Guoqi Qian, Prof. Yuriy Kuleshov and Dr. Tingjin Chu in Jul. 2022. I received my M.S. and B.S. in Mathematics from Peking University on Jun. 2016 and Jul. 2013, respectively.

Softwares

  • R package: edgeR (Empirical Analysis of Digital Gene Expression Data in R)
    We develop the new quasi-likelihood method for differential expression analysis using the exact calculation for unit deviance of Negative Binomial distribution. It is available on R-Bioconductor.

  • R package: statmod (statmod: Statistical Modeling)
    We make the exact distributional calculations for unit deviances of Binomial, Poisson and Negative Binomial distributions. It is available on R-CRAN.

  • R package: IBGS (Iterated Blockwise Gibbs Sampler)
    IBGS is an MCMC search algorithm designed to find the best model in the high dimensional data given a model selection criterion such as AIC, BIC and so on. Moreover, it can identify those most important covariates having the significant influence on the response. It is available on Github.
