About me

I am a research officer in Prof. Gordon K Smyth lab team at Bioinformatics Division, Walter and Eliza Hall Institute of Medical Research (WEHI). I completed my PhD degree in Statistics from the University of Melbourne under the supervision of A/Prof. Guoqi Qian, Prof. Yuriy Kuleshov and Dr. Tingjin Chu in Jul. 2022. I received my M.S. and B.S. in Mathematics from Peking University on Jun. 2016 and Jul. 2013, respectively.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Statistics, School of Mathematics and Statistics, The University of Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, Feb. 2017 - Feb. 2022

  • M.S. in Mathematics, School of Mathematical Science, Peking University, Beijing, China, Sep. 2013 - Jun. 2016

  • B.S. in Mathematics, Yuanpei College, Peking University, Beijing, China, Sep. 2009 - Jul. 2013

Research Interests

  • Bioinformatics: limma, edgeR

  • Model Selection: optimal model in high dimension via Gibbs sampler

  • Model Averaging: preparing the candidate model set in high dimension
